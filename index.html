Ministry of Justice - Find MoJ data

Find MoJ data is a service for finding official data. The service is a facade over Datahub - an open source data catalogue - and is designed to help technical and non-technical users. I helped the team build it, launch a private beta and scale out the service to ~3000 datasets and ~200 active users.
